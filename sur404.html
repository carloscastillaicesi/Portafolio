Sur 404

Sur 404 explores the intersections and bridges between Design and Social Sciences. Therefore, it tackles issues such as privacy, public management, gender, applied social science and co-design.


Our identity is based on the metaphor of an upside-down user as a reminder of the millions of users the designers, polititians and research dont take into account when delivering a service, product or experience.
