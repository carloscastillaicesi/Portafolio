Colors of Cali


“Sometimes there's so much beauty in the world I feel like I can't take it, like my heart's going to cave in.” {American Beauty}

This project is a ode to my city. A beautiful city, in the middle of the mountains, full of beautiful people, nature and music.


I wanted to capture the beauty of it’s daily interactions, registering the vibrant colors that constitutes everyday scenes. Let’s treat every photograph like a movie scene, looking for color palettes!
